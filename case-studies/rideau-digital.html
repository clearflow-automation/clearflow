The problem

Rideau Digital is a 14-person digital marketing agency founded in 2016, managing paid search, SEO, and social campaigns for 47 clients across retail, home services, and professional services in Ontario and Quebec.

Every month, the last week became "reporting week" — and everyone dreaded it. Account managers each manually exported raw data from Google Ads, Meta Ads Manager, and Google Analytics for their assigned clients. Those exports — 47 separate CSV files per platform — landed in a shared drive folder with names like Google_Oct_FINAL.csv and Meta_Natalie_v3.csv.

From there, each file was manually copy-pasted into a master Excel workbook that had grown to 52 tabs and 68,000 rows. The file was 47MB and crashed if two people had it open simultaneously. VLOOKUP formulas referenced sheet names that changed whenever someone added a new client — producing cascading #REF! and #N/A errors that took an additional hour to trace.

The combined monthly reporting effort was 37 staff hours. In two of the previous four months, a client received a report with incorrect cost-per-lead figures — one caught by the client before Rideau did. The owner was personally reviewing every report until midnight on the last Thursday of each month. One senior account manager resigned, citing the reporting process in her exit interview.

The solution

ClearFlow audited the workbook in a 90-minute screen-share and identified three failure points: fragile VLOOKUP chains referencing dynamic sheet names, no validation on the CSV import step, and client report templates embedded inside the master workbook rather than generated independently.

The solution, built and delivered in two weeks:

01

A Power Query pipeline that automatically consolidates all CSV exports from a designated drop folder into a single clean data model — regardless of how many clients are added or removed. No manual copy-paste. No sheet-name references.

02

A VBA macro that loops through the client list and generates all 47 individually formatted PDF reports in a single click — pulling from the Power Query model and applying each client's own template automatically. Total generation time: 4 minutes.

03

A locked input template with protected formula cells and data validation rules — making it structurally impossible for staff to overwrite formulas or paste data into the wrong column.

The first reconciliation run also surfaced $8,400 in campaign hours that had been completed but never billed — invisible inside the old manual workbook.
